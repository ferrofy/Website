    Motto & Core Values

    The motto of the NCC is "Unity and Discipline" (Ekta aur Anushasan). It is a tri-services organization, comprising the Army, Navy, and Air Force, engaged in grooming the youth into disciplined and patriotic citizens. Given in 1957 by 11th Central Advisory Comittee.
